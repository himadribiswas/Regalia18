Regalia 2018

An Octave Of Happiness

Regalia,the annual cultural festival of RCC Institute of Information Technology,is the biggest celebration of music,art and dance in town.It is the place where students from all walks of life can come together and unleash their creative horses. Like every year Regalia will be an amalgamation of the competitions and outstanding performances.Regalia 2018 promises to bring up to the audience the best experience ensuring it to be the most memorable one. Regalia for us is not only an event but a journey to define ourselves,find our ways and most importantly take back a lot from the team. So we call all of you to be a part of this celebration,make our journey bigger and gather this experience all by yourself because this time we are are DREAMING BIG,and we will LIVE BIGGER!

Band Bash

Rules
1. Team Size: 4-6 members. At least 1 Vocalist and 3 different instruments required.Cross College is allowed.

2. Time Limit: 12+3 minutes from empty stage to empty stage (including soundcheck).

3. Songs can be of any language(Bengali, Hindi, English). Any swearing on stage will lead to disqualification.

4. Equipment’s provided will be: 
  i. Adequate Microphones
  ii. Drum Kit

5. All the equipment provided by the college (If any) must be handled with proper care.

6. Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments, Brass section and special effects. However, programmed music is not allowed.

7. A warning bell will be given at the end of 12 minutes.

8. Marks will be deducted for exceeding time limit.

9. Any form of obscenity or inappropriate behaviour with the management, judges, organizers and crowd will result in direct disqualification.

10. The final decision regarding any matter lies in the hands of the organizers and judges and is absolutely binding.

11. Team REGALIA reserves the right to make any last-minute changes in the rules and disqualify a team on grounds of misconduct and obscenity.

Rule the Ramp

Theme

Open Theme

Rules
1. It is a team event. A team can have 10- 15 members. (Including participants, choreographer and coordinator).

2. Time limit for every team would be 10+2 minutes (Including setup and the performance both).

3. Negative marking if participants exceed time limit.

4. A green room would be provided for the changing purpose.

5. Should carry their tracks in C.D, Pendrive Or Aux.

7. Use of cigarettes, alcohol and any unfair means is strongly prohibited.

8. Teams will be judged on costumes, walking stance and attitude.

9. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Foot Loose

Rules
1. Every participant should be a school/college/university student.
More than one group from the same institute is allowed.

2. Each team may have 4-12 members.

3. Time limit is 8+1 minutes for each group.
Negative marking on exceeding the time limit.

4. Selection of dance form is open to the participants.

5. Dance performance should not convey any indecent gesture.

6. Costumes should be decent enough to be worn in a college premise.
Co-ordinators have the right to cancel a performance in case of revealing costumes.

7. Participants are allowed to use props. However, props won't be provided by the coordinators.Participants will have to carry their own props.

8. The participants are requested to bring two CDs to avoid technical disturbance. Out of which,
they are also requested to submit one CD to the coordinator minimum 1hour before the
performance with the group name written on it.

9. The participants will be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formation, synchronization,
expressions, costumes, makeup, setup and overall effects.

10. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
